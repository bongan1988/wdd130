Capetown

Town, located in the Western Cape of South Africa, is one of the country’s leading economic and professional hubs. Historically established as a supply station by the Dutch East India Company in 1652, the city has evolved into a modern center for finance, technology, tourism, and maritime trade. Today, Cape Town is recognized for its growing tech ecosystem, strong entrepreneurial culture, and its strategic role in international commerce through its port and global business connections. The city continues to attract professionals across industries due to its innovation-driven economy and diverse workforce.
