History

RiverStone Adventures was born on the river, forged by rushing water, rugged landscapes, and a passion for exploration. What started as a small crew of dedicated guides grew into a trusted name in white-water rafting, driven by the thrill of the rapids and respect for the river’s power. Every journey reflects our commitment to bold adventure, skilled guiding, and unforgettable moments on the water.
