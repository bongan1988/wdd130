History

RiverStone Rafting Company was born from a deep respect for rivers and a passion for adventure. What began as a small group of outdoor enthusiasts exploring local waterways has grown into a trusted rafting company dedicated to unforgettable river experiences.Our journey started with a simple idea: bring people closer to nature through safe, guided white-water adventures. Over the years, we’ve refined our skills, strengthened our safety standards, and built a team of experienced guides who know the river like home. Every rapid, bend, and calm stretch tells a story—and we’re here to help you experience it.

Today, RiverStone Rafting Company continues to grow with a forward-looking vision. We invest in modern safety practices, eco-friendly operations, and community involvement to ensure that our rivers remain wild and accessible for generations to come.Whether you’re seeking adrenaline, connection with nature, or a shared adventure with friends and family, RiverStone Rafting Company is where the journey begins.
