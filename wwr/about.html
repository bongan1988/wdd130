history

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate river enthusiasts, RiverStone Adventures has grown from a small local outfitter to a renowned name in the rafting industry. Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and unforgettable experiences has earned us a loyal following of adventurers from around the world.
